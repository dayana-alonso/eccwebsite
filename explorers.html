Explorers

Infants

Infants: 6 weeks – 18 months

Infants are explorers!They explore the world physically and using their senses. Our infant environment is designed to encourage curiosity and build on the infants developing skills. Our program is designed with developmentally appropriate experiences and exercises to help your child develop as they achieve different milestones.

Language development is essential during infancy, even before they acquire noticeable verbal skills. Infants have their own way of communicating. The Explorers are taught American Sign Language, to aid non-speaking children in their communication efforts. This helps to facilitate the communication of a child’s basic needs with their parents such as bottle, mommy, daddy, more, etc.

Our trusted teachers are caregivers as well. They learn to read the cues of each child in order to respond properly to their needs. They make sure each baby feels comfortable, safe and secure. Teachers are Infant CPR certified Be assured safety is our top priority.

American Sign Language- Is taught to enhance communication. Sign language paired with works spoken while gesturing this enhances the parent-child communication. Daily reports
- We document all feedings, tummy times, diapering and all other important information about your baby’s day.

What to bring?
  • Diapers
  • Wipes
  • Pacifier (if used by your child) cannot have any attachments
  • A Sippy-cup
  • A minimum of 2 changes of clothes
  • Pre-made bottles for the day (must have covers on them)
  • Baby food in plastic unopened store-bought containers
  • All infants must wear socks
  • All walking/ attempting to walk infants must wear closed-toe shoes.
  • No sandals or backless shoes.
