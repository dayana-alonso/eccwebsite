Pathfinders Care

Pathfinder

Toddlers: 18 months – 2.5 years

    Toddlers are finding their way every day! This is why they are Pathfinders. They are full of energy, continuously working of their independence, developing curiosity about the world around them and learning to interact with their classmates.
    They learn through fun and maintain interest with exciting engaging activities in the classroom and by using the different centers.

    Our curriculum is an exciting program that uses music and movement, verbal cues and other engaging methods to introduce young children to learning in a fun way. Language development is extremely important at this age.

    The children are spoken to frequently, and we encourage them to engage in conversation with their friends and teachers during play and learning experiences. They will be taught American Sign Language to support building each child’s verbal and nonverbal communication skills. They are also taught Spanish on a weekly basis.

Interactive Learning

We encourage learning through music, art, physical activity and sensory play! 

Toddler Development

In addition to creative learning, we also focus on other developmental skills such as potty training , social and emotional development.

American Sign Language

Is taught to enhance communication. Sign language paired with works spoken while gesturing this enhances the parent-child communication.

Daily reports

We document all areas of focus for learning, sign language of the week, Spanish words of the week, meals, snacks, diapering, potty training all other important information about your child’s day.

Teachers are CPR certified- Safety is our top priority.

What to bring?
  • Pull-ups (must open on the sides)
  • Wipes
  • A Sippy-cup
  • A minimum of 2 changes of clothes
  • A blanket
  • Child must wear closed-toe shoes only. No sandals or backless shoes
