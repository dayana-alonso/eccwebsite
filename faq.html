FAQ Page
Question & Answers

We have gathered the ECC's most common questions for you here. If you can't find your annswere, feel free to contact us via Ask Us A Question form.

Q: Are you licensed?

A: Yes! Our center meets and exceeds all safety and licensing requirements by the State of Alabama.

Q: Can I visit my child at the center at any time?

A: Yes, Emi Childcare Center has an open door policy. You can visit your child at any time. We do ask that it not be during the class’ naptime as this can be disruptive.
Parent involvement is wonderful! We welcome it.

Q: How to check for inclement weather closings?

A: Our social media pages are the quickest way to get the information to all families. Visit our website and facebook page
for current information on closings.
We will also notify local media outlets of unexpected closures due to weather. You can also call the center directly at 256 489 4620.

Q: Does the center serve meals and snacks?

A: Yes We Do! For us it's very important that the meals are mad on-site. We serve meals and snacks.

Q: What is the waiting list?
Is there a fee to be added to it?

A: The waiting list is created to ensure that families that are interested in their children being placed in our center as soon as space
is available are able to secure a spot in our program.

There is a non-refundable $25 fee to be put on the waiting list. The $25 fee will be applied to your registration fee of $100. This is due at the time of enrollment. We enroll families as soon as space opens up.

Priority is given to those on the waiting list. We will keep in contact with you to update you on the status of your spot during the process.

Q: How do I add my child to the waiting list?

A: You can call us directly to find out if there is a waiting list 256 489 4620 or send us an email.

Q: Where is Emi Childcare Center located??

A: We are located at 4835 Sparkman Huntsville, Drive, AL 35810.
Near UAH, Woody Anderson Ford, AAA school and not far from Redstone Arsenal.

Q: Interested in applying for a job at our center?

A: You can call us directly for job listings 256 489 4620 or send us an email.
