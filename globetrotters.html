Preschool

Preschool: 2.5 years – 4 years

    Preschoolers are inquisitive, imaginative, and love to learn about new things daily. We aid them in benefiting from opportunities to share, converse, inquire and helping one another.

    Young preschoolers benefit from a variety of activities, freedom to explore and experiment, and engaging units or projects to get involved in. The lesson plans are created in weekly themes and skills, but are still are flexible in order to address each child’s interests and pivotal areas of growth.

    The curriculum is age-specific and made to encourage involvement of all learners. These lessons will be accompanied throughout the week with arts & crafts, story time and songs as part of their preschool curriculum.

    Weekly Spanish

    Our preschool class has Spanish lessons weekly. Our teachers use fun lessons that integrate music with fun activities enriching the lessons.

    Independence

    We encourage children to do things for themselves. This begins with skills such as hand washing, nose blowing, proper use of utensils, and other things.

    American Sign Language

    Is taught to enhance communication. Sign language paired with works spoken while gesturing this enhances the parent-child communication.

    Daily reports

    We document all areas of focus for learning, sign language of the week, Spanish words of the week, meals, snacks, restroom breaks all other important information about your child’s day.

    Teachers are CPR certified- Safety is our top priority.

What to bring?
  • 2 complete changes of clothes (including socks and underwear)
  • A Blanket
